CSC 299
Making Music With Computers

A course exploring the creative side of computing in the context of music, sounds, images, and other digital artifacts.

Open to all majors!

Students will develop several digital artifacts and elementary musical compositions, as well as collaborate on interdisciplinary team projects.

No previous programming experience required

Emphasis will be given to computer programming for music making, live performance, and interaction.
